About Us

Graduating amidst a global pandemic has challenged us all as designers, but with that, is has strengthened us to face challenges head on. At the beginning of the year we were given a brief to substitute our traditional internship experience with a show that addressed our growth as designers. There were a lot of new and unfamiliar decisions that we had to make, but we came together as a class and saw the project through [something sentimental].

One aspect we felt was essential to our show was to highlight the members of our class both as a group of thriving designers, as well as talented individuals. Here you can find information about each of us, including our sites, social, and personal videos addressing: what inspires us as designers, what design means to us, and what will stick with us after we graduate.
